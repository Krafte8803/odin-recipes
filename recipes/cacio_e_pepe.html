Spaghetti aglio e olio

It can not become more easy!

Only four Ingredients. So make sure you get good quality

EFeast like the ancient roman shepherds in the romanian hills.

Ingredients

  • one pack of quality pasta (tonnarelli if you can find them)
  • some high quality Pecorino Romano
  • fresh graded pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil some water, just enough, and season to taste with salt.
  2. Add your pasta to the boiling water.
  3. Grate the cheese and place it in a bowl. Wood or plastic works best.
  4. As soon as the pasta is al dente toss it in the cheese and immediately stir like crazy.
  5. If needed add a little bit of pasta water.
  6. Add the pepper and keep stirring.
  7. Place on a plate and garnish with Pecorino and pepper.
