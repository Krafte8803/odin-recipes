Spaghetti aglio e olio

An all time classic if you did not go to the groceries!

An easy and quickly created symphony based on olive oil and pepper.

Enjoy the italian flair in a matter of minutes.

Ingredients

  • one pack of quality pasta (preferably spaghetti)
  • a considerable amount of olive oil (extra vergine and not the cheap stuff!)
  • sea salt (also not only a little bit)
  • pepper (and please freshly grinded)
  • basil (The green, living plant. No dried stuff!)
  • garlic (fresh for sure)
  • some parmigiano reggiano
  • one fresh red chilly

Instructions

  1. Boil some water, just enough, and season to taste with salt.
  2. Add your spaghetti to the boiling water.
  3. Preheat a pan and place a generous amount of oil in it. As soon as you can smell the olive oil, reduce the heat to a minimum.
  4. In the meantime, cut the chilly in to fine slices. Having a sharp knive helps alot.
  5. Do the same to the garlic and place both in the now hot olive oil.
  6. Now your pasta should be al dente and you place it in the oil, too.
  7. Season with freshly grinded pepper to taste and give it a good stir. Pro Tip: A splash of starchy pasta water gives it a twist and some creamy texture)
  8. Now quickly chop the basil, place the pasta on a plate and garnish with basil and parmigiano.
